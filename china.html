GENERAL CHINESE PROGRAM (GCP)

This program is designed to meet the needs of job market in Cambodia. It is a tailored program started from beginner to advance made to help students to communicate daily with the emphasis on four macro skills such as reading, writing, listening, and speaking. Student will be able to use Chinese with their job with confidence.
