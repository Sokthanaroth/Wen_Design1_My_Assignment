Foreign Language Programs

The Department of Foreign Languages (DFL) is a major department providing several language courses, non-degree language programs in ACLEDA Institute of Business and communitywide. The department promotes languages and culture competencies for all. It has a holistic approach to student learning and development and is committed to build on what students know, take account of different learning styles and rates of learning. We promote a safe, stimulating and harmonious learning environment to encourage all students to achieve their full potential. The syllabus is regularly evaluated with highly professional experts.

Goal and Membership

For many esteemed universities/Institutes, an alumni relations strategic plan is a non-negotiable part of maintaining contact with students after they pass out.
The plan considers not just a way of keeping in touch with the alumni but also setting tangible goals that:

Promote the universities/Institutes community locally, regionally and globally.

Recognize the experiences, needs, and prospects of alumni to improve universities/Institutes standards continually.

Promote a culture of philanthropy among the alumni to support the universities/Institutes.
