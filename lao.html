LAO PROGRAM

The Intensive Lao Course (LC) is a non-assessed, non-academic course especially prepared for ACLEDA Staff and public with a focus on improving general English skills. The program has been designed to develop the Lao proficiency of ones who wish to master their four macro skills-listening, speaking, reading and writing.
